Questions, answered

Is the trial really free?

Yes. Every plan opens with a full 14-day trial that unlocks every Pro feature — AI replies, automation, branded quotes, all of it. No credit card to begin, and you can cancel anytime before it ends.

Do I need a credit card to sign up?

No card to get started — just your email. You explore QuoteFleet in full first. A card is only ever kept on file so your service continues seamlessly if you decide to stay, and you can add it whenever you're ready.

Can I cancel anytime?

Anytime, in one click from your dashboard — no emails to send, no one to call. Cancel before the trial ends and you're never charged a cent.

Are quotes really unlimited?

Send as many quotes as you like on either plan. There are no per-quote fees and no monthly caps — quote every lead, every time, without watching a meter.

What's the difference between Vital and Pro?

Vital gives you everything to quote, capture leads, and follow up. Pro adds the automation that works while you don't: AI auto-reply with 24/7 customer chat, branded PDF quotes, full follow-up automation, your own custom domain, analytics, and priority support.

Will I get any surprise charges?

Never. Your plan is one simple monthly price — no per-quote charges, no hidden add-ons. You're only billed when your trial ends, on the plan you chose, and you can switch between Vital and Pro whenever you like.
