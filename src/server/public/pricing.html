FAQ

Common questions

Can I bring my own Anthropic API key?

Yes — on Pro and Fleet plans. Save costs and keep all AI calls under your account.

Do you charge for white-glove install?

Free on Pro and Fleet. $149 one-time on Starter. We can install on WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, or custom HTML.

What happens after the trial?

Your dashboard goes read-only. No charges, no surprises. Pick a plan whenever you're ready.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes — Pro and Fleet support custom domains via CNAME (e.g. quote.yourcompany.com).
