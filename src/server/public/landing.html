FAQ

Common objections, answered.

Will my rates be public?

No. Rates are private by default. Marketplace exposure is opt-in.

Do customers need an account?

No. Customers submit quote requests directly from your site or hosted quote page.

Can I use my own rates?

Yes. Edit rates in the dashboard or import rate cards, zones, and accessorials.

Does it replace my TMS?

No. It captures and quotes inbound website leads before they become manual work.
