FAQ

Simple answers before you start.

Do I need a website?

No. You can start with your own branded link and share it directly.

Can I add it to my website later?

Yes. Start with the hosted page first, then embed it whenever you want.

Do customers need an account?

No. They simply open your page and use it.

Can the page match my company branding?

Yes. The hosted page can be customized for your company style.

Can customers ask questions?

Yes. An optional AI chat widget can answer questions and guide next steps.

Do I need a contract or complicated setup?

No. It is designed to be quick to start and easy to use.

Can I control who gets access?

Yes. You decide how and with whom the calculator link is shared.

Are my rates shown publicly?

No. Your calculator is shared only through the links and access you choose.

Does it work with my current TMS or workflow?

Yes. QuoteFleet is a standalone branded link — with an optional website embed — that works alongside whatever you use today. There's no TMS integration to set up and nothing to replace. Share it by link or embed it, and keep running your business exactly as you do now.

Why we built QuoteFleet

Every promise in writing. No testimonials we made up.

We're a small team, and we won't pretend to be more than we are yet. QuoteFleet exists because quoting freight by hand is slow, and every carrier deserves a branded rate page without hiring a developer. Instead of invented reviews, here's exactly what we guarantee — each one you can hold us to.

  • 14-day all-inclusive trial

    Every feature unlocked for 14 days. A card is on file so there's no interruption if you stay — cancel before it ends and you pay nothing.

  • Cancel anytime

    No contracts and no lock-in. Leave whenever you want and keep every quote and lead you've already made.

  • No hidden fees

    One simple monthly price. No per-quote charges, no surprise add-ons, and no setup fees.

  • Unlimited quotes on Vital

    Quote as much as you run. Our entry plan has no quote cap — you're never metered on the work you do every day.

  • Your data stays yours

    Every account is isolated at the database. We never sell your data and never train AI models on your rates or leads.

  • Marketplace is opt-in

    You're only listed in the carrier marketplace if you choose to be. It's off by default and free to join.

  • Free rates database

    Anonymous, aggregated rate data we build from every quote — it gets sharper as more carriers join, and it's free for every carrier.

Transparent about what we are — and what we aren't.

Your rates live in per-tenant isolation enforced at the database, we never sell or train on your data, and we don't claim certifications we don't yet hold. It's all written out in plain language.
