Support

How can we help?

Use this page to route account, quote setup, billing, security, and data requests to the right place. For the fastest help, include your company name, tenant link, quote reference ID, lead reference ID, and screenshots when relevant. Do not send passwords, secret API keys, database URLs, or payment card details.

General support

Account setup, rate-card import, public calculator questions, or dashboard issues.

support@quotefleet.net

Security reports

Vulnerability disclosure, suspicious access, data isolation concerns, or security questions.

security@quotefleet.net

Legal and data requests

DPA, privacy, export, deletion, correction, or data-processing questions.

legal@quotefleet.net

Sales and launch help

Carrier onboarding, pilot launch review, setup review, or production readiness planning.

hello@quotefleet.net
Important: QuoteFleet support will never ask for your password or full secret keys. If a support request needs access to a tenant account, use the dashboard, a temporary test tenant, or a non-secret screenshot.

What to include

Request typeHelpful details
Quote or lead issueTenant link, quote reference ID, lane, equipment, accessorials, and what result you expected.
Rate setup issueService, equipment, rate card label, accessorial code, zone name, and whether the issue affects public quotes.
Login or signup issueEmail used, company name, tenant slug, browser/device, and approximate time.
Data requestCompany name, authorized requester name, tenant slug, and requested action such as export, correction, or deletion.
Security issueRoute affected, impact, reproduction steps, and whether customer data may be involved.

Expected response targets

  • Urgent availability, security, or data exposure issue: same-day operator review.
  • Account access, billing, or production quote-flow issue: 1 business day.
  • Setup help, rate import, or general product question: 1–2 business days.
