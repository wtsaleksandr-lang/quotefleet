# QuoteFleet — Brand & Domain Candidates

> Scope: 15-25 brandable domain candidates for an embeddable instant-quote calculator + AI dispatcher targeting US/Canada drayage and trucking carriers. Goal: regular registration under ~$50/yr (no premium auctions). Compiled May 2026.

**Methodology disclosure.** This research environment cannot reach WHOIS, RDAP, or registrar availability APIs directly (403 / blocked DNS). All availability calls below are **inferred** from web-search signals: presence/absence of an active website at the domain, brand chatter, trademark hits, and competitor name collisions. Every "likely available" line should be **manually re-checked at Namecheap, Porkbun, or Cloudflare Registrar** before you commit. Where a sibling TLD is clearly in use (e.g. `loadagent.co`, `quotedeck.io`, `ratepilot.app`), that increases the likelihood that the corresponding `.com`/`.io`/`.ai` is also held.

**Pricing reality (critical).** As of Feb-May 2026, .ai pricing went from $70 → $80/yr with a **mandatory 2-year minimum** on new registrations (so $160 upfront). **That is above your $50/yr ceiling** for any .ai. Treat .ai as optional — don't make it a hard requirement. .io is ~$30 first-year / $50+ renewal at most registrars, right at the edge. .com at Cloudflare/Porkbun is ~$10-11/yr, squarely in budget.

## Why these top picks

**`haulpilot`** is the strongest signal. "Haul" anchors it in trucking; "pilot" reads as both "captain" and "AI co-pilot" — perfect for an AI dispatcher product. No active company, no app, no trademark hit. Two syllables, easy to spell on a phone call, ends in a hard consonant. The only reason it's not a guaranteed win is we couldn't run WHOIS — non-zero squatter chance.

**`quotefleet`** survived the audit cleanly. Descriptive ("quotes for fleets"), and "fleet" is the noun your buyers identify with. Mild risk: literal/descriptive marks are weaker for trademark protection than coined ones (haulpilot is more distinctive).

**`drayagent`** is the strongest *drayage-specific* play. If your wedge is "instant drayage quotes for port carriers," this name signals it instantly. Drayage is a more concentrated and lucrative niche — owning the word in the name is leverage.

**`lanevue`** is your "elegant brand" hedge. If you ever pivot from utility ("get a quote") to intelligence ("understand your lanes"), the name supports that. Less obvious but more brandable long-term.

**`drayquoter`** is the safety net if `drayquote.com` (taken) being unavailable kills the cleaner version.

## Stay away from

**Direct competitors / active products**: `ratepilot.app`, `drayquote.com`, `draybot.com`, `drayrates.ai`, `mydrayrate.com`, `loadagent.co`, `freightai.us/.ca/.systems`, `quotedeck.com/.io/.app`, `quotebot*` (saturated), `drayinsight.com`, `draymate.com`, `drayeasy.com`, `draydex.com`.

**Active non-freight brands at the .com**: `haulwire.com`, `milepay.com`, `freightdash.com`, `trustload.com`, `instaload.com`, `ratecalc.com`, `quoteyard.in`.

**Big trademarks — never imitate**: Project44, Loadsmart, FourKites, Convoy (defunct but TM lives), Freightos, Truckstop, Maersk, Uber Freight, C.H. Robinson, **Freightquote** (C.H. Robinson — do **not** put "freightquote" in your brand), DAT, McLeod, Loadpilot (now AscendTMS).

**Generic dictionary words you can't defend**: `quotr`, `rateo`, `quoty`, `loadrate`, `ratecalc`, `freightquote*`. Weak trademarks, bad SEO.

## How to actually buy it

1. **Verify for real.** Search the top 5 at `porkbun.com` and `domains.cloudflare.com`. Spot-check `who.is/whois/<domain>` for parked records.
2. **Buy at Cloudflare Registrar or Porkbun** for the .com. Cloudflare sells at-cost (~$10.44/yr renewal, free WHOIS privacy). Porkbun (~$11) has a friendlier UI.
3. **If you also want .io**, buy same-day — ~$50/yr renewal, still under budget.
4. **Skip .ai for now.** $160 upfront (2-yr minimum) is over your $50 ceiling. Defensive grab later if the brand wins.
5. **DNS at Cloudflare regardless of registrar.** Set NS to Cloudflare for free DDoS + edge cache + email routing.
6. **Lock + TOTP 2FA the registrar account** the moment you buy.
7. **5-min USPTO check**: search `tmsearch.uspto.gov` for the bare word in classes 35 + 39 (business services / transportation).
8. **Grab social handles same hour** — X, LinkedIn, Instagram. Squatters scrape new .com registrations daily.
