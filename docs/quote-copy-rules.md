# QuoteFleet copy rules

Use this guide for small copy edits across public, dashboard, and support docs. The goal is to keep QuoteFleet messaging consistent without changing product behavior.

## Core positioning

QuoteFleet is a branded hosted rate calculator page for trucking service providers.

Preferred framing:

- `companyname.yourquote.net`
- branded rate calculator page
- no website changes needed
- no contracts
- quick setup
- optional AI chat
- branded PDF quotes
- share by link, email signature, or message

## Approved message themes

Use language that emphasizes:

- customers can open the branded rate page anytime
- providers can share a direct quote link without rebuilding their website
- setup is lightweight and practical
- quotes can be saved or shared as branded PDFs
- AI chat is optional, not required

## Avoided wording

Avoid wording that mispositions the product or overpromises:

- `quote desk`
- `private rates` in hero or social copy
- `Turn website visitors into freight quote leads`
- `Quote freight faster from your website`
- unsupported speed, conversion, or revenue statistics
- claims that require customer volume, carrier integrations, or operational proof not already shown in the product

## Copy review checklist

Before changing user-facing copy, confirm that the copy:

- explains the hosted branded page clearly
- keeps the no-website-change message visible
- avoids unsupported statistics
- treats AI chat as optional
- mentions branded PDF quotes only where relevant
- does not imply a feature that is not already built

## Safe support-work rule

Copy polish should stay low risk. Do not change pricing logic, quote calculation, API behavior, database schema, authentication, payments, or AI workflow behavior as part of a copy pass.
